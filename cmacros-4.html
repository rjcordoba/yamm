Op. booleanos

  1. opb|| | &&
  2. restbopb exp restb | ε
  • exp en s1 exp s2 representa cualquier cadena de caracteres entre s1 y s2
  • ε representa la cadena vacía.
Zn = exp1 || exp2;
  1. Z.b1 = exp1;
  2. Z.b2 = exp2;
  3. Zn = Z.b1 + Z.b2;
Zn = exp1 && exp2;
  1. Z.b1 = exp1;
  2. Z.b2 = exp2;
  3. Zn = Z.b1 * Z.b2;
Zn = exp1 && exp2 opb exp3 restb;
  1. Z.resa = exp1 && exp2;
  2. Zn = Z.resa opb exp3 restb;
Zn = exp1 || exp2 || exp3 restb;
  1. Z.reso = exp1 || exp2;
  2. Zn = Z.reso || exp3 restb;
Zn = exp1 || exp2 && exp3 restb;
  1. Z.resa = exp2 && exp3;
  2. Zn = exp1 || Z.resa restb;
